Our Philosophy

The Art of
Mindful Hospitality

At Hachi Ju Hachi, we believe that a great meal is more than food on a plate — it is an experience of warmth, presence, and craft. Our chefs bring the finest ingredients directly from Japan, prepared with techniques passed down through generations.

We named our restaurant after the number 88, a symbol of good fortune and abundance in Japanese culture. Every evening, we gather around the table to share that abundance with you.

Thursday Evenings

Chef's Selection

おまかせ

Surrender to the chef. Each Thursday, we receive the finest fresh fish flown directly from Japan. Ask for the Chef's Selection — a freestyle dinner with no menu, where our chef curates every course to its peak perfection. No two meals are alike.
